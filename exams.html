📘 Competitive Exams Details

Get exam info, eligibility, syllabus, and more

🇮🇳 UPSC – Civil Services

  • Eligibility: Graduate
  • Age: 21–32 years
  • Pattern: Prelims + Mains + Interview
  • Posts: IAS, IPS, IFS, etc.

📄 SSC CGL / CHSL

  • Eligibility: 12th (CHSL) / Graduate (CGL)
  • Age: 18–32 years
  • Pattern: Tier-I to Tier-IV
  • Posts: Clerk, Inspector, Auditor

🏦 Bank Exams

  • Eligibility: Graduate
  • Exams: IBPS, SBI PO/Clerk
  • Pattern: Prelims + Mains + Interview
  • Jobs: PO, Clerk, SO

🚆 Railway RRB

  • Eligibility: 10th / 12th / Graduate
  • Posts: ALP, Technician, NTPC
  • Pattern: CBT 1 & 2 + Skill Test
  • Department: Indian Railways

🪖 NDA – Defence

  • Eligibility: 12th Pass
  • Age: 16.5–19.5 years
  • Exam: NDA by UPSC
  • Jobs: Army, Navy, Air Force

👩‍🏫 Teaching – CTET / TET

  • Eligibility: D.El.Ed / B.Ed
  • Exams: CTET, State TET
  • Posts: PRT, TGT, PGT
